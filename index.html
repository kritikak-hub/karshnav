The Living God Bhairav: Karshanav Shakya

Karshanav Shakya is a young boy from Kathmandu, Nepal, who became the Living God Bhairav at just 3 years old. This means people believe he represents the god Bhairav, who is seen as a protector. Karshanav Shakya is a symbol of hope and spirituality for many people in Kathmandu.

Despite being a Living God, Karshanab leads a life like any other child. He attends school, spends time playing with his friends, and resides with his family. However, he holds a particularly unique role. He rides in a chariot with the Kumari (Living Goddess) and Ganesh (another Living God) during major celebrations like Indra Jatra. Visitors come to seek his presence, offer prayers, and receive blessings.
